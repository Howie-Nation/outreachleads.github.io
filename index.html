FAQ

Common Questions

How does the free trial work?

We set up your campaign and run it for two weeks at no cost. You receive verified leads and outreach is sent on your behalf. At the end of the trial you decide whether to continue — no pressure either way. You keep all leads delivered during the trial.

Do I need to give you access to my email?

No. We set up a dedicated outreach inbox on your behalf so your main domain stays completely protected. Interested replies forward directly to your real inbox automatically.

Do you guarantee results?

We guarantee delivery — verified leads sourced and outreach sent every week. Reply rates depend on your offer and market. Industry average for targeted cold outreach is 2–5% positive replies, which we aim to meet or exceed.

How do I communicate with you?

Everything is handled over email. No calls required. We keep things efficient and fully documented for both sides.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Cancel with 14 days written notice via email. No long-term contracts.
